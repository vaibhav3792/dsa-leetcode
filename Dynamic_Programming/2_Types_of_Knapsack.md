## 0 1 Knapsack Problem
- Subset Sum
- Equal Sum Partition
- Count of Subset Sum
- Minimum Subset Sum Difference
- Target Sum
- Number of subset and given difference


## Types of Knapsack
1) Fractional Knapsack (Greedy)
2) 0 1 Knapsack
3) Unbounded Knapsack

---
- Weights and Values

- Can break in fractional knapsack. No need for DP. Greedy is used.
- Can't break in 0 1 Knapsack.
- Multiple occurences allowed in unbounded knapsack unlike the other two.
